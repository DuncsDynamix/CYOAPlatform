## RESTRICTED OPERATIONS DEFINITION {#slide-35}

\'Restricted operations\' means any work which may involve direct or potential contact with untreated sources of underground water,

with partially or fully treated water within water treatment works,

or any surface of an operational asset (including those temporarily out of use) which will itself be in contact with potable water at any stage in its distribution to the point where it is made available to consumers.

Principles of Water Supply Hygiene Document, Revisions: Technical Guidance Notes

## RESTRICTED OPERATIONS {#slide-37}

DO NOT WORK ON RESTRICTED OPERATIONS IF: 

- You have persistent vomiting or diarrhoea
- You have a prolonged unexplained fever
- You have Cryptosporidiosis
- You have jaundice
- You have Hepatitis A or E
- You have dysentery
- You or a member of your family have been diagnosed with typhoid/paratyphoid
